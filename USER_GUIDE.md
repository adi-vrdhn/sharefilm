## 1. üìΩÔ∏è Add Movie

**Send movie recommendations to your friends!**

### How to Use:
1. Click the **‚ûï Plus Button** to open the form
2. **Search for a Movie:**
   - Type the movie name in the search box
   - Autocomplete suggestions will appear as you type
   - Click on a movie to select it
3. **Select Friends:**
   - Check the boxes next to friends you want to send the movie to
   - You can select multiple friends at once
   - The counter shows how many friends you've selected
4. **Preview:**
   - See the movie poster before sending
5. Click **Submit**

### Special Features:
- **Already Watched Warning:** If a friend has already watched the movie through Discover, you'll get a popup
  - **Cancel:** Don't send the movie
  - **Still Send:** Override and send it anyway
- **Bulk Sending:** Send to multiple friends simultaneously
- If sending to multiple friends and one has watched it, you can choose to continue with the remaining friends

## 3. ‚ú® Discover

**Swipe through movies and build your watchlist!**

### Two Modes:

#### Browse Mode (Default)
**Find movies based on your preferences:**

1. **Set Your Filters:**
   - **Genres:** Select multiple from dropdown (Action, Comedy, Drama, etc.)
   - **Platform:** Choose streaming service (Netflix, Prime, Disney+, etc.)
   - **Language:** Select from English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and more
   - Click **Find Movies** to apply filters

2. **Search Similar Movies:**
   - Type a movie name in the "Search a movie" box
   - Select from autocomplete suggestions
   - Click **Find Similar** to get recommendations
   - Click **Back to Browse** to return to normal mode

3. **Swipe Through Movies:**
   - **Swipe Left** or click **Watched:** Mark as watched
   - **Swipe Right** or click **Nah:** Skip this movie
   - **Tap Card** or click **Want to Watch:** Add to watchlist
   - **‚Üê Back Button:** Go back to previous card

4. **Smart Features:**
   - Fresh movies load every time you visit
   - Recommendations based on your watched history
   - Movies you've watched appear in similarity searches
   - 30% recommendations, 70% filtered content

#### Watchlist Mode
**View and manage your saved movies:**

1. Click **Watchlist** tab
2. **Filter Your List:**
   - By Genres (click chips to toggle)
   - By Platform
   - By Language
3. **View Cards:**
   - See all movies you've marked "Want to Watch"
   - Click **Remove** to delete from watchlist

## üÜò Need Help?

### Common Issues:

**Movies not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear filters and try again

**Can't find a friend?**
- Make sure their username is exact
- They must have an account
- Check spelling

**Discover showing same movies?**
- The page now auto-refreshes on each visit
- Try changing your filters
- Use the Similar Movies search for variety

**Movie Party not saving?**
- Ensure all fields are filled
- Check date is in the future
- At least one friend must be selected
